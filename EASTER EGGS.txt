 - EASTER EGGS -

NOTE: There are a few Easter eggs hidden throughout the game that you can discover.
      If you wish to find them yourself DO NOT READ this document.


































1) You can find the hidden dev room by clicking on the twenty-sided die in the library, once you enter you can talk to our avatars and take part in an tabletop RPG session.

2) If you fail the cult puzzle and head back to the kitchen you will see Heckles and Chuckles fighting again.

3) You can beat the final puzzle by selecting Mongolian Death Worm instead of whatever you picked at the beginning of the game leading to special dialogue.

4) The ending newspaper has various reference to whatever outfit you currently had on after you beat the game.
